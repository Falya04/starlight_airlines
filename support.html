FAQs

  • Q: How can I check my flight status?
    You can visit our "Flight Information" page to check real-time status updates.
  • Q: Am I allowed to eat outside food and drinks on board?
    Passengers are not allowed to bring and eat outside food and drinks on board.
  • Q: How can I cancel or change my booking?
    You can contact our support team via phone or email for assist with cancellations or changes.
  • Q: Are pets allowed on flights?
    Yes, only small pets are allowed in the cabin with the permission.
  • Q: Can I carry medication on board?
    Yes, you need to declare all medications at the check-in counter and also bring the doctor's letter.
  • Q: What documents should I provide for my flights?
    Malaysian adults are required to provide their original identity cards for domestic flights. Malaysian children may be required to provide their original birth certificates before they are allowed to board.
  • Q: What happens if there is a flight change after I've checked in?
    All passengers need to check the Flight Information as soon as they arrive at their departing terminal for the latest info on their flights such as boarding gate number and scheduled time of departure.
  • Q: My baggage is too small to be checked in. Can I carry it with me onboard?
    Only one piece of baggage may be hand-carried onboard provided that it does not exceed the dimensions of 54cm x 34cm x 20cm and does not weigh more than 8kg. The piece of hand-carry baggage must fit under the seat in front of you or in an enclosed storage compartment in the cabin.
  • Q: Is there a limit to the weight of each piece of baggage?
    Regardless of which checked-baggage allowance category you purchase, each piece of checked baggage cannot exceed 34kg in weight.
  • Q: What is the purpose of the live chat function?
    Live Chat is a new digital customer service platform that facilitates real-time communication with our support team through a chat interface. It provides a convenient means for customers to seek assistance or resolve issues.
