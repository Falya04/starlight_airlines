Baggage Policies

Carry-On Baggage:

  • Allowance: One carry-on bag and one personal item.
  • Weight Limit: 7 kg (15 lbs).
  • Dimensions: Maximum size of 56 x 36 x 23 cm (22 x 14 x 9 inches).

Checked Baggage:

  • Economy Class: 1 piece, 23 kg (50 lbs), dimensions must not exceed 158 cm (62 inches).
  • Business Class: 2 pieces, 32 kg (70 lbs) per bag, same dimensions as Economy.
  • Excess Baggage Fees: Additional charges apply for extra or overweight bags.

Special Items:

  • Sports Equipment: Allowed with extra fees if exceeding limits.
  • Musical Instruments: Small instruments allowed in carry-on; larger instruments must be checked.
  • Strollers and Baby Items: Complimentary check-in for strollers and car seats.

Prohibited Items:

  • Flammable items, sharp objects, firearms and weapons and chemical and toxic substances are not allowed.

Tips for Passengers:

  • Arrive at the airport early for baggage check-in.
  • Clearly label your bags with contact information.
  • Drink plenty of water before flight to prevent yourself from dehydration.
  • Stay calm to comfort yourself by adjusting your seat for good posture, move regularly such as stretch your body and stay entertained by reading a book or watching download movies.
  • Double check all of the belongings items after landing.
